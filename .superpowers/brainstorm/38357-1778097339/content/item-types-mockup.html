monitor-value
Shows current readings as compact text. Clean, minimal. Works well when you just need a quick sanity check.
- type: monitor-value name: Mac Studio adapter: dashdot url: http://192.168.1.10:3001 metrics: [cpu, ram, gpu] interval: 10
✓
monitor-graph
Shows a sparkline per metric with the latest value below. Lets you see trends at a glance without opening anything.
- type: monitor-graph name: Mac Studio adapter: dashdot url: http://192.168.1.10:3001 metrics: [cpu, ram, gpu] interval: 10
